Bumper Car Programming

Use the program Bumper Car for your bumper car.  This program will make the robot drive straight until it bumps into something, then back up a little, make a right turn, then go straight again (and repeat forever).

 

Challenges

  • The large bumper is designed to press the touch sensor when various different types of obstacles are hit, but you may still find cases where the bumper does not work or gets hung up on an obstacle.  Can you improve the bumper to prevent any problems you encounter?

  • The Bumper Car program has a very simple strategy to try to avoid obstacles.  Try modifying the program to experiment with different strategies.  For example, you might try alternating turning left and right, or turning different amounts.  You could also add a sound or something each time the bumper hits something.
