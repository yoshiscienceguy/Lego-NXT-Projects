Inch Worm Programming

Use the program Inch Worm for your Inch Worm. 

 

Using the Inch Worm

Use the large black gear to turn the motor by hand until the inch worm lies flat against the floor and has all four wheels touching the ground before running the program.  The inch worm should always be flat before you start it.

 

Challenge

Can you figure out what makes the inch worm moves forward?  What do all the different gears do? 
Does this give you any ideas for any other designs of your own?
