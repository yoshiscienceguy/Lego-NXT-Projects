Front Wheels

Important: Make sure the wheels are oriented correctly, with the "spokes" on the inside.

Note that the front wheels on Multi-Bot do not have the rubber tires installed.  This allows the robot to turn easily on smooth surfaces by sliding the front wheels from side to side.  Only the rear wheels, which are driven by the motors, need the traction provided by the rubber tires.

Multi-Bot will fit the NXT brick with either AA batteries or the NXT Rechargeable Battery Pack.

After pushing the NXT brick down onto the blue pegs in the front as shown above, push the two gray bushed pegs in from the sides to lock the NXT in, then connect the two wires to ports B and C as shown below.

This completes the Multi-Bot vehicle base with wheels and a "medium" gear ratio.  To change to treads or a different gear ratio, and to learn how to easily change AA batteries, see the Vehicle Variations.
