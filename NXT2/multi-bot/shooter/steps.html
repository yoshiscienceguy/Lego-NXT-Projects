Attach the Ball Shooter Arm to Multi-Bot, and connect the shooter motor wire to port A on the NXT.

Multi-Bot with the Ball Shooter Arm attachment can be controlled by the Remote Control feature in the NXT 2.0 software, or by programs running on the NXT.

The Ball Shooter Arm attachment can be combined with other Multi-Bot sensor attachments.
