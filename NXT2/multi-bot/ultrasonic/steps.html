Multi-Bot Ultrasonic Sensor
   
 

 

Building Instructions

1

2

The Ultrasonic Sensor can be attached to the inside of the front attachment bar on Multi-Bot as shown below.  Attach the wire to port 4 on the NXT.  The wire can be pushed into one of the "wire clips" between the ball-end pegs on Multi-Bot's front upright to keep it out of the way.

Or, the Ultrasonic Sensor can also be attached to Multi-Bot's rear attachment bar as shown below. 

Or, when the Double Touch Sensor Bumper attachment is attached to Multi-Bot, the Ultrasonic Sensor can be attached to the white cross beam on top of bumper attachment, as shown below.
