5 Button Remote Control
Building:
Program:

This simple remote control is designed to control another NXT creation by Bluetooth wireless remote control.  With two touch sensors and the three NXT buttons, there are a total of five buttons that can be used. 
 

The buttons are positioned so that you can operate the touch sensors and NXT buttons at the same time, with your fingers and thumbs.
 		 The 5 Button Remote Control can control projects such as the Race Car, or other projects that you create.
