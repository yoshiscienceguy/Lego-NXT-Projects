Electric Guitar
Building:
Program:

Try your hand at playing blues and rock music with a guitar that makes music using the NXT�s speaker!
Use the special sound files provided to download realistic electric guitar "power chord" sounds to your NXT!

The ultrasonic sensor measures the distance to a plate that slides along the neck of the guitar, which determines which note or chord to play. Pressing the orange button on the NXT plays the note or chord (similar NXT 1.0 version is shown above).
