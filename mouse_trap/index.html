Mouse Trap
Building:
Program:

Like a traditional mouse trap, this NXT Mouse Trap quickly snaps closed when the mouse steps on the plate when trying to get the bait.  However, this trap is computerized and motorized, so it can reset itself automatically if it senses that the mouse was missed or escaped, and get itself ready for another try.
