Top Spinner Programming

Use the program Motor A Full for your spinning top.

Tip: The Motor A Full program simply turns on motor A at full power and leaves it on until you stop the program with the dark gray button on the NXT brick. This program can be used to power any creation you make that just needs to keep one motor spinning.

 

How to Use Your Top Spinner

Insert the shaft at the end of the gear train into the top of the spiral worm gear, and hold your top lightly against a hard floor (don't press down). Run the program to start the top spinning.  When it has finished speeding up, gently lift the motor off straight up.  The worm gear may fall off during launch.  That's OK, just replace it before the next run.

How it Works

The NXT Motor turns too slowly to spin a top by itself, so the gears speed it up.

The high-speed outer shaft (pictured here on the left) spins 15 times as fast as the NXT motor!

The medium-speed inner shaft (closer to the orange motor hub)  spins 5 times as fast as the NXT motor. You may want to try this one if your top is large or heavy.
 

 

Challenges

  • The top shown above spun for 35 seconds on a wood floor after launch. Can you beat that?

  • Build multiple tops and see how many you can get spinning all at the same time before one stops.
