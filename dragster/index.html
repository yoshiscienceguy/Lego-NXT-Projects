Dragster
Building:
Program:

This "top fuel" dragster uses all three of the NXT motors to achieve maximum power.  It will pop a wheelie at the start then run straight down the hall!
 

An adjustable wheelie bar lets you control how high the wheelie at the start will be and how long the dragster will hold the wheelie.  Gears are used to speed up the NXT motors to achieve more top speed during the run.
