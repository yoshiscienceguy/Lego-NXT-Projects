Three Stages to Better Photos of LEGO Creations


These pages give advice for taking better pictures of LEGOs in three stages, depending on how much effort you want to put into it and what equipment you have or are willing to buy.  It may take a little learning and experimenting in the beginning, but once you figure it out, you will be able to quickly take great pictures of all of your LEGO creations (click sample pictures to enlarge).
