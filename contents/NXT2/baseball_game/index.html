Baseball Game
Building:
Program:

This Baseball Game has an automatic pitching machine, a batter you control by pressing the touch sensor on the wire, and a catcher.  If you set the game up facing a wall at the right angle, the pitched balls will launch outward then bounce off the wall and come back towards the batter.  Some pitches will be strikes and would be caught by the catcher, and some will be "balls" too far to the left or right and miss the catcher.  Can you get a hit, or a walk (4 balls), before you miss 3 strikes?  Just like real baseball, it is harder than it looks.  The balls come at you quickly and you only have a split second to decide between a ball and a strike.
